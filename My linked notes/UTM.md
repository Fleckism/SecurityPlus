Unified threat management ([[UTM]]) refers to a 
- security product that centralizes many types of 
- **security controls—
	- firewall, 
	- anti-malware, 
	- network intrusion prevention, 
	- spam filtering, 
	- content filtering, 
	- data loss prevention, 
	- VPN, 
	- cloud access gateway—into a single appliance**.
